COMPANY PROFILE

Overview

Fresh-En Co., Ltd. is an environmental systems company established on January 21, 2021. The company is based in Namyang-eup, Hwaseong-si, Gyeonggi-do, Korea, and operates across environmental systems manufacturing, supply and related services.

Company Overview

Fresh-En manufactures and delivers air-conditioning equipment, industrial blowers and exhaust devices, gas filters, distillers, heat exchangers, gas generators, kitchen hoods and automatic control systems. It also operates in business support services, machinery and equipment wholesale, and home appliance retail.
