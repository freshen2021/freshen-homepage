Greeting

Fresh-En is committed to delivering integrated environmental systems across development, manufacturing, supply, installation and maintenance. Leveraging its manufacturing capability in air-conditioning equipment, industrial blowers and exhaust devices, gas filtration equipment, kitchen hoods and automatic control systems, the company provides practical solutions tailored to customer operating environments.

Responsible ManufacturingWe consider both product quality and supply stability from the earliest stage of development and production.
Field-Oriented ThinkingWe look beyond installation to actual operation, maintenance requirements and sustained performance.
Long-Term RelationshipsWe value long-term trust and reliable after-sales support over one-off delivery.

CEO Philosophy

Fresh-En aims to provide efficient systems, dependable supply and responsible maintenance support in line with changing standards and customer requirements. Through field understanding, technical capability and disciplined execution, we seek to be a trusted partner in environmental systems.

CEO PhilosophyWe strengthen a delivery model that provides site-fit environmental systems with quality and reliability, while taking responsibility through post-installation maintenance.
Corporate DirectionWe aim to become a trusted environmental systems partner through technical expertise and practical execution.
Execution PrincipleWe build lasting relationships through continuous support that extends beyond installation.
