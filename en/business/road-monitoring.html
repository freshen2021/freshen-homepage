Road Surface Monitoring

Fresh-En supports road-surface monitoring systems that improve operating awareness in weather-sensitive outdoor environments.

Scope of Work

Road-surface monitoring combines pavement sensing, weather inputs and monitoring logic to support operational decisions.

Surface Condition MonitoringObservation of road-surface change and operating conditions.
Weather IntegrationUse of temperature, humidity, precipitation and related inputs.
Response SupportPotential linkage to anti-icing response systems.

Applications

These systems are designed for roads, airport areas and other outdoor environments where pavement condition matters.

Road NetworksMonitoring for public roads and key transport corridors.
Airport AreasApplications for runways and airport access routes.
Anti-Icing LinkageUse alongside anti-icing systems where required.
