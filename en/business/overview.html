BUSINESS

Business Overview

Fresh-En’s business portfolio is built on manufacturing capability in environmental systems and expanded through project execution, controls, sensing, installation and long-term support.

Core Business Fields

Fresh-En delivers indoor air quality systems, wastewater treatment, infrastructure systems and monitoring/control solutions, as well as installation, commissioning and maintenance support.

Indoor Air Quality SystemsVentilation, dust collection and air cleaning for occupied indoor spaces.
Wastewater Treatment SystemsHigh-strength wastewater treatment and low-strength water reuse systems.
Infrastructure SystemsMonitoring, control and system integration for tunnels, airports and roads.

Delivery Structure

The company connects design, manufacturing, supply, installation, commissioning and maintenance into one integrated project delivery model.

Manufacturing BaseProduct and system capability grounded in registered manufacturing activities.
Execution CapabilityProject delivery that extends from supply to field implementation.
Long-Term SupportPost-installation maintenance and operational support as part of the service model.
