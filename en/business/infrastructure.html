Infrastructure Systems

Fresh-En supports tunnels, subways, airports, roads and other infrastructure environments with integrated monitoring, sensing and control systems.

Scope of Work

Infrastructure projects combine mechanical, electrical, sensing, monitoring and automatic control elements into one operating structure.

Monitoring & ControlReal-time monitoring and coordinated control for critical assets.
InstrumentationMeasurement systems for operating status and environmental conditions.
MaintainabilityProject planning that accounts for operation, inspection and maintenance.

Operational Perspective

Infrastructure assets require systems designed not just for installation but for continuous operation and fast response.

Automatic ControlFlexible control strategies aligned to the operating profile of each site.
Maintenance SupportLifecycle thinking that includes inspection, maintenance and field service.
ScalabilityExpandable integration of sensors, instruments and control devices.
