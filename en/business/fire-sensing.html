Special Fire Detection

Fresh-En supports fire-detection solutions tailored to technically demanding spaces where general-purpose detection may be insufficient.

Scope of Work

Detection methods and system configurations are selected according to space characteristics, environmental conditions and response requirements.

Special-Environment ResponseApplication to spaces such as warehouses, tunnels and utility corridors.
Early WarningSupport for earlier detection in technically challenging environments.
Integrated ResponseConnection of detection, alarm and control functions.

Applications

These solutions are suitable for spaces where early warning and detection reliability are especially important.

Logistics & IndustryWarehouses, industrial sites and complex facility environments.
Infrastructure SpacesTunnels, utility corridors and related enclosed spaces.
ReliabilityDesign consideration for dependable detection and timely response.
