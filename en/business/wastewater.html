BUSINESS

Wastewater Treatment Systems

Fresh-En designs and delivers systems for high-strength wastewater treatment and low-strength water reuse to improve treatment stability and operating efficiency.

Scope of Work

Wastewater projects are planned according to influent characteristics, treatment targets and operating conditions.

High-Strength WastewaterSystems designed for challenging, high-load wastewater conditions.
Low-Strength Water ReuseReuse-oriented configurations that improve resource efficiency.
Operational StabilitySystem planning that considers maintainability and long-term operation.

Applications

The company supports industrial, utility and public environments where reliable water treatment performance is essential.

Industrial PlantsTreatment systems aligned to plant-specific wastewater characteristics.
Power & Public UtilitiesStable operation for facilities that require dependable process performance.
Reuse-Oriented ProjectsConfigurations that support reuse and operating-cost efficiency.
