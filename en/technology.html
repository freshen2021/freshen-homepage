TECHNOLOGY

Technology

Fresh-En responds to diverse site conditions with technical capability built through the development, manufacturing, supply and implementation of environmental systems.

Technology Capability

The core of Fresh-En’s technical capability is its ability to balance configuration suitability, supply stability and field applicability.

Manufacturing CapabilityWe secure quality and stability through in-house capability in environmental systems and related equipment manufacturing.
Supply SystemWe build flexible supply structures to match each project and procurement model.
Field ResponseWe respond to installation conditions, operating environments and maintenance requirements.
Operational SupportWe provide technical support that extends through commissioning and maintenance.

Application Range

These capabilities are applied across indoor air quality systems, wastewater treatment, infrastructure systems and monitoring-related projects.

Indoor EnvironmentsSolutions for schools, healthcare facilities, residential buildings and other occupied spaces.
Water TreatmentSolutions for high-strength wastewater treatment and low-strength water reuse.
InfrastructureSolutions for tunnels, airports, roads and integrated monitoring/control environments.
