Technology That Changes Environments

Fresh-En delivers integrated environmental systems across indoor air quality improvement, wastewater treatment, infrastructure monitoring and control, road-surface monitoring, anti-icing and emissions monitoring. By connecting design, manufacturing, supply, installation, commissioning and maintenance in one delivery model, we provide practical solutions built for real operating environments.

BUSINESS

Fresh-En’s business portfolio spans environmental systems that improve indoor environments, support industrial water treatment and enhance operational stability across infrastructure assets.

Indoor Air Quality Systems

Ventilation, dust collection and air cleaning solutions for school kitchens, healthcare facilities, multi-use buildings and other occupied indoor spaces.

Wastewater Treatment & Reuse

Systems for high-strength wastewater treatment and low-strength water reuse designed to improve treatment performance and operating efficiency.

Infrastructure Monitoring & Control

Integrated systems for tunnels, airports and roads, including monitoring, sensing, automatic control, anti-icing and emissions monitoring support.
